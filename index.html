About Me

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Zhulin An and Prof. Yongjun Xu.
My recent works are mainly on knowledge distillation, representation learning and image classification.
